Oppa Bear Pixels are a collection of 2,000 random Generative NFTs on Optimism, Layer 2 of the Ethereum Blockchain. Oppa Bear NFTs consist of a variety of different unique body, clothes, hats, backgrounds, accessories and colors. There are also 20 rarities and 10 super rarities Oppa Bear NFTs, which are completely fabulous.
