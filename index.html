Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What are Gangnam Tiger Pixels?

Gangnam Tiger Pixels are a collection of 2,000 random Generative NFTs on Optimism, Layer 2 of the Ethereum Blockchain. Gangnam Tiger NFTs consist of a variety of different unique body, clothes, hats, backgrounds, accessories and colors. There are also 20 1/1 unique Gangnam Tiger NFTs, which are completely fabulous.

What is the story behind Gangnam Tiger?

Gangnam Tiger is a story of a white tiger who only wanted to dance in Gangnam style. He wants to be famous dancer and become a well-known fashion designer.

What is the objective of this Gangnam Tiger Pixel project?

We want to spread out the world to see the Gangnam dancing style by using Gangnam Tiger as the main characteristic itself. Gangnam Tiger Pixels are much more than an NFT art project. We want to build up not only the community, but rather the “family”. The community holders can share their ideas and suggestions for better improvement.

What are the benefits of holding this Gangnam Tiger Pixel?

We reward our Gangnam Tiger community by giving community rewards and utility with collaboration with other artists, and much more. The other benefits will be announced later on.

We plan to roll out 3 phases: First HODLERS Pre-sale for 100 WLs, Community Pre-sale for 300 NFTs, Public sales for 1,400 NFTs. The remaining about 200 WLs will be distributed to founder, artists, developers and marketing purposes. The dates are not yet announced. Please stay tuned for more update in our Discord and Twitter.
